About Rural Vikas Bharat

Rural Vikas Bharat (RVB) is an industry-oriented policy impact simulation system developed to predict the socio-economic effects of government policies before implementation. It focuses on rural development, welfare schemes, subsidies, and agriculture-related policies. By using citizen demographic data and rule-based simulation logic, RVB generates analytical insights that support data-driven governance and effective policy planning.
